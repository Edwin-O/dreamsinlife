About Me

My name is Edwin Ochoa, I'm 16 years old and I was born and raised in L.A.
I enjoy all types of music; if it sounds good, I'll listen to it.
Writing is a hobby of mine, whether I'm writing what's in my head
or I'm just writing down nonsense, it relaxes me. If I can't find
anything else to do, I just go outside, wander off and explore my
city, or just take a bus until I get somewhere; it really doesn't
matter where, as long as I have nice or new experiences each day
