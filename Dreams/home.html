Kendrick Lamar

Role Model

There are many reasons to look up to Kendrick Lamar,
but one of the main reasons is because of how much
I can relate to him as a person, and how much he's
accomplished despite the obstacles placed in front
of him. He raps not for the money, but because it
is his passion and writing helps him destress, just
as writing down what I'm thinking does to me.
He speaks with plenty of wisdom, which is rare for
someone his age to have. When he talks, almost all
the things he says get me thinking about life. He's
always emphasizing hard work gets you to where you
want to go. Despite his current status, he stays
humble and is constantly seeking self improvement.
